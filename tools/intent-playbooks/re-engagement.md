# Re-engagement

They already raised a hand once: a no-show, a closed-lost, a stalled deal. Not cold, and it must not read cold. The frame is picking back up, not meeting for the first time. Reworks pull 7-14% because the relationship already exists.

## Needs

- A CRM export of no-shows, closed-lost, and stalled prospects, emails re-validated.
- A true reason-now: something that changed on our side since the last touch.
- Vars: `{{last_touch}}`, `{{reason_now}}`.

## The play

Run the frame: prior interaction, then one line of context, then the offer, then a reason-now, then a low-friction CTA. The reason-now is the engine, something that makes now different from last time: "we just helped a similar {{niche}} book 40 in six weeks," or a new capability. Without it, it is just a nag. The context must be true, since they remember the last conversation and a wrong detail kills it. Runs evergreen on a quarterly export; validate emails first, because people changed jobs since.

## Subject lines

The subjects below are examples; write fresh to the play.

Warm, picking-back-up, not a fresh pitch.
- "circling back, properly this time"
- "{{company_name}} + us, take two"

## The plan

| Variant | Opener | Offer | Proof / risk reversal | CTA |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1A | the prior interaction | the offer again | the reason-now | low-friction ask |
| 1B | a "since we last spoke" | the improved offer | a fresh result | share the update |
