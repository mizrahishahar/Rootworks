# Campaign 6: Sales & Revenue Leaders x All Verticals

**Segment:** FR Persona = Sales / Revenue, across all B2B SaaS/tech verticals EXCEPT Cybersecurity and Services & Non-Tech.
**Persona:** Hired revenue leaders (CRO / VP Sales / RevOps) who own the number, not owners.
**Deliverable wording:** "שיחות מכירה" (cross-vertical). Gender-neutral recipient language.
**Structure:** Email 1 (1-A role call-out). Email 2: 2-A value + video, 2-B referral/handoff (TEST). Money results-based, not "only". Light CTAs.
