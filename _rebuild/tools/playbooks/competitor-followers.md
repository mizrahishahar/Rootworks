# Competitor-followers

The follow is the whole hook: they follow a competitor, a tool, or a category page, so they are already aware of the problem you solve. Soft intent, but a real pattern break.

## The play

- **Lead with the follow** — "saw you follow {{page}} on LinkedIn, looked at alternatives yet or still evaluating?" It reads as observation, not surveillance, because a follow is public.
- **Body** — the offer as the alternative or the upgrade, with the proof that separates you from {{page}}.
- **CTA** — a soft compare, low commitment.
- Exclude current and former employees of the followed company before you send; emailing them is the embarrassing miss here.
