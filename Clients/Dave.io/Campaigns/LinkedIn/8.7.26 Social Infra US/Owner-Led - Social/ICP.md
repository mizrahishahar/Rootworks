# Owner-Led - Social - ICP

**Segment:** owner-led, no dedicated infra owner. Founder / CTO at small, early-stage US companies on the cloud, sourced from engaging with infra content on LinkedIn.

## Social discovery - Search Posts

Time range: Last Month (refresh weekly)
Keywords (max 6): platform engineering, developer productivity, cloud cost, scaling infrastructure, DevOps as a service, SRE
Engagement types: Comments, Reactions, Shares
Add later, sharper than keywords: Specific Creators + Specific Companies (competitor pages), names TBD

## Audience - the prompt to find the right people

From the people who engaged with those posts, keep only the technical decision-maker at early-stage, cloud-based US software companies: CTOs, co-founders who serve as CTO, technical co-founders, and founders, at C-level or founder seniority in engineering, at 10 to 200 employee companies (pre-seed through Series A), running on AWS, GCP, or Azure, headquartered in the United States (Canada acceptable). Do not keep individual engineers, DevOps hires, or non-technical roles. One person per company.

## Structured backup (filters)


Company:
  headcount:    11-200
  technologies: AWS OR GCP OR Azure
  hq_location:  United States (+ Canada)
Contact:
  seniority:    CXO, Founder
  department:   Engineering (leadership only, no Engineer/Developer/SRE/DevOps sub-departments)
Strictness:     strict on persona + location, flexible on keywords + engagement

(No industry filter. No hiring gate, the topic engagement is the signal.)
