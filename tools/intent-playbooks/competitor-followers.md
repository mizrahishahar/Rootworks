# Competitor-followers

The follow is the whole hook: they follow a competitor, a tool, or a category page, so they are already aware of the problem you solve. Soft intent, but a real pattern break.

## Needs

- A follower export of a chosen page, ICP-filtered.
- Current and former employees of the followed company excluded.
- Vars: `{{page}}`, `{{company_name}}`.

## The play

Lead with the follow, framed as observation not surveillance, because a follow is public: "saw you follow {{page}} on LinkedIn, looked at alternatives yet or still evaluating?" The offer is the alternative or the upgrade, carried by the proof that separates you from {{page}}. The CTA is a soft compare, low commitment. Exclude current and former employees before you send; emailing them is the embarrassing miss here.

## Subject lines

The subjects below are examples; write fresh to the play.

Reference the space, not the competitor by name in the subject.
- "alternatives to {{category}}?"
- "quick one {{first_name}}"

## The plan

| Variant | Opener | Offer | Proof / risk reversal | CTA |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1A | the follow, noticed | the alternative | what separates us | still evaluating? |
| 1B | evaluating {{category}} | the upgrade | a switch proof | soft compare |

## Templates
