# Piper AI — Cold Email Sequence v1

**Target.** VPs / Directors of Pre-Construction, Chief Estimators, Directors of Estimating at US construction firms, $20M–$200M revenue. Mid-market GCs, heavy civil, design-build, large MEP/specialty trade contractors.

**CTA split.**
- 1A → free live-bid-package analysis (soft ask, send-us-your-RFP)
- 1B → 90-second video walkthrough
- 1C → numbered checking-in, soft case-study reference
- 1D → free live-bid-package analysis (different hook: win-rate / margin)
- 2A → re-up free live-bid-package analysis with same-day turnaround framing
- 2B → re-up video CTA
- 3A → soft re-engagement on the free analysis
- 3B → referral ask ("who owns bid review at {{company_name}}?")

**Anchor case study.** `{{CASE_STUDY}}` — placeholder. **Piper must fill this in.** Format: one specific customer outcome with named firm, project type, and time-to-decision delta. Example shape: *"Helped {customer name} cut bid review from 3 weeks to 6 hours on a $40M federal pursuit."* Without a real customer result, leave the line empty and lean harder on the free-analysis CTA.

**Send timing.** Email 1 Day 1 → Email 2 Day 4 → Email 3 Day 9.

**Personalization slots.**
- `{{first_name}}` — prospect first name
- `{{company_name}}` — prospect company name
- `{{FIRST_LINE}}` — Flowroots writes per-prospect opener (recent project, post, hiring signal, etc.) before launch
- `{{CASE_STUDY}}` — Piper fills in their best customer result line

**No em dashes anywhere.** Construction vocabulary only: bid package, RFP, drawings, specs, contracts, addenda, exclusions, pursuit, pre-con, estimator, win rate, margin, section 7.

## Notes for Piper before launch

1. **Fill `{{CASE_STUDY}}`.** This is the single most leverage-heavy edit. One concrete customer outcome line, real firm name, real project type, real time delta. Without it, 1A/1B/1C/2A all weaken. If you have nothing fully public, give us the strongest internal one and we'll get it cleared with the customer.

2. **Decide on the free-analysis logistics before send.** When a VP Pre-Con replies with a live RFP, Piper needs a defined intake (where do they send the package, who runs it, what's the turnaround promise, who delivers the brief). The email promises 24 hours. If the real turnaround is 48, change the copy. If you can't honor any turnaround, swap the CTA to the video.

3. **`{{FIRST_LINE}}` is Flowroots' job.** Per-prospect opener, manually researched from the prospect's LinkedIn, a recent firm pursuit, a posted project win, or a hiring signal. We don't AI-template it.

4. **CTA testing.** The four Email 1 variants point to three distinct CTAs (live-package analysis, video walkthrough, soft check-in). After 2 weeks of sends, we'll see which CTA pulls. The winning CTA gets scaled across variants in v2.
