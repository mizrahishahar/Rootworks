## What Dave is

An on-demand, AI-powered cloud infrastructure and DevOps team. Dave becomes a company's **infrastructure owner** without them hiring one, delivering senior-level infra and DevOps output from day one.

## How it works (the mechanism)

- Dave's AI plugs into the customer's **tech stack** - their cloud, Git, ticketing system, and communication channels.
- It **reads the cloud and never writes to it**, so nothing changes on autopilot and the environment stays secure.
- It builds a **"context lake"** that automatically maps the entire infrastructure and cloud layer in minutes - which is what lets Dave deliver value immediately instead of the 3-6 months it takes to ramp a hire.
- A human **Remote Deployed Engineer (RDE)** checks and signs off on **every change before it ships**. The customer's own approver (CTO / VP Eng / Director of DevOps) typically does a ~60-second final sign-off.

## The value (three pillars)

**Availability, productivity, and a fraction of the cost.** The team ships product instead of getting buried in infrastructure, and scales output without scaling headcount. The contrast that lands: **~$2,500/mo with Dave vs. a ~$200-250K/yr infra hire** that also takes months to ramp and may carry recruiting fees.

## Who to reach (ICP)

- **US (primary) and Canada**, tech companies running on a major cloud (**AWS / GCP / Azure**).
- **Titles:** VP Engineering, CTO, technical founder, Head of Infrastructure / Platform.
- **The real filter is not company size - it is whether they have an infra owner, and whether they have a bottleneck or are hiring.** Two teams both fit: a 30-person company with no infra engineer, and a 300-person company where one or two DevOps are bottlenecking 50 engineers.
- **Skip:** pure developers who do not own decisions, and non-technical roles.

- **Infra ownership gaps** - titles/profiles showing infra/platform/SRE responsibility landing on people who are not dedicated to it.
