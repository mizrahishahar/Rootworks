## When to do it

Daily, Morning

## What we get

- LinkedIn engagement & relationship building
- D100 prospects moving through the pipeline.

## Process

### 1. Engage after post (if posted)
Right after the post goes live, spend ~15 minutes engaging from the post. Reply to early comments, like and comment in your feed, respond to DMs.

### 2. Check limits
- [Sent invitations](https://www.linkedin.com/mynetwork/invitation-manager/sent/) — confirm pending invitations are under 100 for the week.
- [New connections](https://www.linkedin.com/mynetwork/invite-connect/connections/) — note everyone who accepted since yesterday.

### 3. Process yesterday's acceptances
For every new acceptance, check Airtable for a matching D100 prospect.

If matched, update their d100 status to 'accepted' and engage their LinkedIn: like 1–2 of their recent posts, comment on one (tag their name), follow the company page.

### 4. Send today's connection requests

Pick **3 d100 companies**, Send a connection request to at least the main contact at each.
Send more linkedIn connection requests (80% ICP, 20% strategic network) until you got to 20.
