# Referral

Borrows warmth: open as if a shared name or a peer sent you. Only ever with a real connection; a fabricated referrer is the fastest way to burn trust and get reported.

## The play

- **Opener** — "{{referrer}} mentioned you might be the person for this", or "your peers at {{peer_companies}} keep raving about {{thing}}." The name does the work; keep it natural.
- **Body** — the offer, carried by the proof the referrer would vouch for.
- **CTA** — soft and warm, the tone of a warm intro, not a cold pitch.
- Never fabricate the referrer or the raving. With no real connection, use another playbook.
