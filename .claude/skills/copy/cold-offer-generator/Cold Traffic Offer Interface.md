# Cold Traffic Offer Interface

The exact structure of every Cold Traffic Offer.md file this skill produces. Two sections, in this order. Never invent proof, customers, or guarantees. Use only what exists in the source data.

---

## 1. Cold Traffic Offer

The proposition. This is the line that goes into cold copy.

> *[The full offer, one paragraph. Outcome, mechanism, risk reversal, differentiation if relevant. Written in the buyer's language, not internal language.]*

**Headline variants** — two or three alternative phrasings of the same offer for testing.

---

## 2. Pillar Assessment

The offer assessed against the Fazio three pillars.

| Pillar | Rating | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| New Money | Strong / Weak / Absent | What new revenue or result this creates for the decision maker specifically |
| Done For You | Strong / Weak / Absent | What is handled so the prospect lifts nothing |
| Risk Reversal | Strong / Weak / Absent | Guarantee or performance model |

**Verdict.** One paragraph: is this shippable to cold copy as-is, does it need sharpening, or is it not viable. If sharpening: what specifically.
