# Hiring-signal

A job post is a company telling you where it hurts. Anchor to the exact role and the gap it leaves open right now, aimed at the decision-maker who owns the outcome, never the recruiter filling the seat. Intent is high, because they just declared the need in public.

## Needs

- A weekly job scrape, filtered to the past 7 days and the target roles.
- Expansion to the decision-maker by company size; recruiters and HR excluded.
- Vars: `{{role}}`, `{{company_name}}`, the `{{function}}` the offer covers.

## The play

Name the role in the opener so it is unmistakably not mass mail: "saw {{company_name}} just posted a {{role}}." Then the ramp-gap pain, the offer as the faster path to what the hire is for: "instead of waiting three months for them to ramp, we can start {{outcome}} this week." The CTA is start-now, before the seat is filled and the pain is masked. Aim by company size, Founder at a small shop, Head or Director at mid, VP at enterprise; the recruiter is a dead end.

## Subject lines

The subjects below are examples; write fresh to the play.

Reference the role, internal and un-salesy.
- "{{role}} at {{company_name}}?"
- "saw you're hiring"

## The plan

| Variant | Opener | Offer | Proof / risk reversal | CTA |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1A | the role, named | fill the pipeline while they ramp | a similar team we run | worth a quick chat |
| 1B | the ramp-gap pain | start this week vs a 3-month ramp | the risk reversal | soft ask |

## Templates

**Email 1 (1A)**
> Hey {{first_name}}, saw {{company_name}} just posted a {{role}}.
> A lot of teams in your space bring us in to handle {{function}} while the new hire ramps. Instead of waiting three months for them to get up to speed, we can start {{outcome}} this week.
> Worth a quick chat?

## Examples

Real, LeadBird production:
- "Hey {{first_name}} - saw {{company_name}} just posted a {{role}} position. A lot of teams in your space bring us in to handle outbound while the new hire ramps up. Instead of waiting 3 months for the new rep to ramp, we can start filling your pipeline this week. Worth a quick chat?"
