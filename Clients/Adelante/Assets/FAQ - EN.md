**What is it, exactly?**
A managed AI support agent for ecommerce. It resolves tickets end to end - checks orders, processes refunds and returns, fixes shipping and delivery issues, edits orders - inside your own stack. Not a chatbot that answers FAQs. An agent that finishes the work. We build it, launch it, and operate it.

**How is that different from the AI already in my helpdesk?**
Those answer. This one acts. It takes approved actions inside your systems, gated by per-action rules you control. And it is operated, not installed: every week we review real conversations, catch drift, and tune it. It improves every day instead of decaying after setup.

**How long until it is live?**
10 days from the mapping call. Days 1-3 we map your workflows and connect systems, days 4-7 we train and test in draft mode, days 8-10 we launch and monitor.

**What is draft mode?**
Before anything reaches a customer, the agent writes the reply and your team approves it. You watch it work before it flies solo.

**What does it cost?**
From $800/month, including 5,000 AI messages. No setup fee. Usage scales with volume, and anything beyond is scoped with you before it is charged. Final price depends on ticket volume, channels, languages, integrations, approved actions, and security requirements.

**Is there a contract?**
No setup fee, no long-term contract. 30-day money-back guarantee if you are not happy with performance.

**What does it connect to?**
Store: Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento. Helpdesk: Zendesk, Gorgias, Freshdesk, Glassix, Crisp. Plus shipping, payments, and your policy sources. Unlimited integrations are included.

**Will it replace my support team?**
No. The UK beauty brand in our flagship case study cut no staff - the agent took the repetitive queue so the team could handle exceptions, product questions, compliance and investigations. It makes your team senior, it does not replace them.

**What happens when it cannot handle something?**
It escalates to a human with full context and suggested next steps. No "let me transfer you" reset where the customer starts over.

**Does it work in Hebrew?**
Yes, natively - correctly gendered and idiomatic, not translated-sounding. Hebrew and English both.

**What results have you actually gotten?**
A UK beauty ecommerce brand doing 1,200-1,500 orders/day: 80%+ of all tickets now resolved end to end by the agent, and their public Trustpilot rating went from 3.5 to 4.0. Fold: 100% of initial customer responses handled by AI within five weeks of launch. Modibodi: 50% of inquiries resolved with no human. Papaya (60+ stores, Israel): 200-400 of every 1,000 inquiries close with zero human involvement. Across deployments, first response drops under 10 seconds with 24/7 coverage.

**So will I hit 80%?**
⚠️ **Do not promise a number.** Results vary by ticket mix. Point at the case-study numbers as proof and set targets on the call.

**Is it secure?**
SOC 2 Type II and GDPR. Scoped access, and an audit trail on every action.

**Can I see it work?**
Yes - we can put a version of the agent built on your own brand in front of you. (See `WhatsApp Bot Demo.md`.)

**What channels does it cover?**
24/7 across email, chat and social messaging.

**Does it only do support, or does it sell?**
Both. It answers pre-sale questions instantly - sizing, availability, shipping times, product comparisons - and recommends products from your live catalog, with order attribution. Buying-intent conversations close in the moment instead of dying in a queue overnight.
