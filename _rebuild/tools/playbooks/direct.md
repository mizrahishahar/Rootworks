# Direct

The default: a plain, specific pitch that earns the reply on the strength of the offer. Use when the offer is clear and no signal drives the outreach. Every other email playbook is a variation on these five parts.

## The play

- **Subject** — earns the open, nothing more. Natural ("Quick question {{first_name}}") or the outcome in plain words ("8 calls in 60 days or you don't pay"). Follow-ups thread under it, no new subject.
- **Opener** — about them, an ask not a lecture. A specific how-you-found-them or a role call-out. Never "Most {{industry}} struggle with...".
- **Body** — the concrete gap as a number, the proof or risk reversal closest to the segment, the mechanism only when the outcome reads skeptical. "You" outweighs "we". Under ~50 words.
- **CTA** — one, value-based, already-done, an asset we can send. No calendar link on the first touch unless the brief's CTA is a call.
- **Follow-ups** — rephrase the offer, route to the decision-maker, add a piece of value. Never re-introduce; "following up" is banned.
