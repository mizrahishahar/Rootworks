## 2. Give Shahar access to manage them (Porkbun Authorized User)

This lets Shahar manage the DNS and mail records needed for sending. He cannot transfer or unlock your domains, so you keep full ownership the whole time.

First, both accounts need this (Porkbun requires it before sharing):

- Verified email, confirmed phone number, and 2FA enabled (app-based or a security key).
- Shahar's Porkbun username is: **shaharmizrahi**

Then, on your (Tamir's) account:

1. Log into Porkbun.
2. Open the ACCOUNT menu (top-right) and choose "Authorized Users".
3. Enter Shahar's username, shaharmizrahi, and click Submit. Porkbun emails him a confirmation.
4. Shahar opens that email and clicks "Accept Access".
5. Done. You can view or revoke access anytime from the same Authorized Users page.

Notes:

- Never share your password. The Authorized User flow is the safe path, no credentials change hands.
- Authorized Users get full DNS and hosting management but cannot unlock domains or start transfers.
