## Section 2: About Your Business

  

**Who do you help? What result can you get them? Is there a specific timeframe? How do you get these results?**

  

We help independent and growing moving companies across North America that are losing revenue to disorganization, manual processes, and disconnected tools. Our ideal customer is a moving company owner or operator managing between 2 and 20 trucks who is doing between $500K and $10M in annual revenue. They are typically running their business across a mix of spreadsheets, WhatsApp, paper forms, and anywhere from 3 to 7 separate tools that do not talk to each other.

  

The result we get them is more closed jobs, higher revenue per job, and a business they can actually manage and scale. Our anchor customer, Cactus Moving, went from $2M to over $7M in annual revenue and scaled from 1 city to 6 cities and from 13 trucks to 51 trucks — all while running on MovePlnr. Their monthly move volume grew from 372 to over 1,095 moves. Their close rate consistently runs between 70% and 93% on qualified leads.

  

In terms of timeframe, most operators see meaningful improvement in lead capture and quote speed within the first 2 weeks. Close rate improvements typically show up within 60 days as pipeline visibility and follow-up automation kick in. Revenue growth compounds from there as dispatch efficiency improves and analytics surface what is actually working.

  

We get these results by replacing every disconnected tool with one closed-loop platform. Pipeline management captures every lead from every source automatically. AI-powered quoting generates margin-protected estimates in seconds instead of hours. Dispatch optimizes crew and truck assignments. Automated follow-up sequences keep leads warm without manual effort. Analytics track revenue, conversion, and rep performance across the whole business in real time. Every action in one stage trains the system for the next.

**What EXACTLY do you sell to your Target Market?**

  

MovePlnr is a purpose-built operating system for moving companies. It replaces every disconnected tool an operator is currently using — CRM, quoting, dispatch, payroll, follow-up, analytics — with one platform where every workflow connects to the next.

  

We sell a monthly subscription per location, structured around move volume:

  

- **Free** — up to 10 moves/month. No credit card required. Full platform access. No time limit.

- **Starter** — up to 30 moves/month at $100/month.

- **Growth** — up to 100 moves/month at $249/month. Most widely used plan.

- **Scale** — unlimited moves at $500/month for high-volume operators.

  

No long-term contracts. Cancel any time.

  

**What the platform does:**

  

- **Pipeline management** — Every inbound lead (phone, web form, live chat, email, SMS) is automatically captured, classified, and queued. Nothing disappears.

- **AI-powered quoting** — Generates detailed, margin-protected estimates in seconds. Accounts for labour, truck size, distance, floor count, and special items. Send by email or SMS in one click. Customers accept from their phone.

- **Dispatch** — Full crew and truck management from one dashboard. Assignments sent via app. Day-of communication logged. Real-time updates when anything changes.

- **Automated follow-up** — Timed email and SMS sequences run automatically between first contact and booking. Quiet leads are flagged.

- **Analytics and reporting** — Revenue by period, close rate by rep, conversion by lead source, payroll breakdowns, Google review performance — all current, all in one place.

- **Live add-ons** — Storage coordination with AI-assisted unit sizing, Stripe Connect payments, Google Business Profile review collection, RingCentral telephony, Tawk.to live chat, full payroll management, built-in BDR sales tools.

  

This is not a CRM with extra features bolted on. It is the only platform built from the ground up for how moving companies actually operate.

**What are some PROBLEMS your ideal clients have that you solve?**

  

1. **Leads disappear before anyone follows up** — The average moving company loses 40–50% of inbound leads because there's no system to catch them. MovePlnr captures every lead from every channel automatically, queues it, and makes sure nothing is invisible.

  

2. **Quotes take hours and pricing is guesswork** — Most operators write estimates manually in spreadsheets or Word docs, hours or a full day later, with no margin protection. MovePlnr generates a professional, margin-protected estimate in seconds — faster response lifts close rates, protected margin improves profitability on every job.

  

3. **Dispatch runs on WhatsApp and paper** — Crew assignments happen in group chats. Job details live on printed sheets or whiteboards. When something changes mid-day, the communication chain breaks down. MovePlnr gives coordinators a real-time dispatch dashboard and movers their assignments through the app.

  

4. **Every tool operates in a different silo** — The typical operator runs separate tools for CRM, quoting, invoicing, payroll, and communication — none sharing data. Every handoff is a potential error or something dropped entirely. MovePlnr connects all of it so information flows through the whole business automatically.

  

5. **No visibility into what is actually working** — Most moving company owners can't tell you their close rate, best lead source, average job value, or top-performing rep — because the data is scattered. MovePlnr surfaces all of it in real time.

  

6. **Growth breaks everything** — The tools that work at 5 trucks fall apart at 15. WhatsApp dispatch can't manage 40 jobs a day. A shared Google Sheet can't handle 6 cities. MovePlnr scales from one location to many without rebuilding anything.
