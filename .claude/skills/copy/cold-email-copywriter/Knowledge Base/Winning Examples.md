# Winning Examples

Real cold emails that worked brilliantly in production. They are here for inspiration: see what landing copy actually looks like, and notice that everything in this skill is thumb rules, not law. Once the foundations are solid (short, specific, sells the reply, value CTA, real proof), you can drift from the patterns here and there, if you have good reasons to. These examples show you exactly how far, and where, that drift still works.

## KEY OBSERVATIONS

Notice across ALL these winning scripts:

1. **Length** — Most are under 60 words, many under 40
2. **Specific brand names** — Taylor-Dunn, Cushman, Figs, NASA, Drake, Stevie Wonder, Castleflexx
3. **Specific numbers** — "$50k saved", "142% YoY growth", "$25K to $250K", "$5M in 90 days"
4. **Direct questions** — "Are you...", "Curious, are you...", "Looking for..."
5. **Value-based CTAs** — video, mockups, demo, catalog, sample, valuation, reel (never "quick chat")
6. **Specific personalization** — references to actual companies, locations, products
7. **Pattern interrupts** — "I'll cut the fluff and get right to it:"
8. **Conversational** — sounds like a real person, not a marketing brochure
