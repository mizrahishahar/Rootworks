# Assets Interface

The per-company ammo the inbox manager deploys: the value it gives in replies, follow-ups, and especially pre-call touches. Matched to the moment, never dumped as homework.

Location: Prospects/{Client}/Assets/INBOX-MANAGEMENT/Assets.md

For each asset, one line: what it is, what it shows, and the moment it fits. Keep it a usable index, not a library catalogue.

## Sections

- Videos and Looms: the link, what it shows, key timestamps, and the moment to send it.
- Case studies: the link, whose story it is, and the doubt it kills (this will not work for me, the scale will not work).
- FAQ answers: the questions prospects actually ask and the one-line answer to give in our own words, with the asset as backup.
- Proof numbers: the verified figures, with their source, that anchor the reason-to-call.

Rules: every asset is offered as help tied to what they just said, never as homework. If the right asset for a moment does not exist, note the gap for the client rather than forcing a weak one.
