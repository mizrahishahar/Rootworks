# Re-engagement

They already raised a hand once: a no-show, a closed-lost, a stalled deal. Not cold, and it must not read cold. The frame is picking back up, not meeting for the first time. Reworks pull 7-14% because the relationship already exists.

## The play

Pick back up, with a reason now.

Open on the prior interaction, one line of context, then the offer again, carried by a reason-now: something that changed on our side since the last touch ("we just helped a similar {{niche}} book 40 in six weeks", or a new capability). Without the reason-now it is just a nag. The context must be true, since they remember the last conversation and a wrong detail kills it.

## Needs

- A CRM export of no-shows, closed-lost, and stalled prospects, emails re-validated.
- A true reason-now: something that changed on our side since the last touch.
- Vars: `{{last_touch}}`, `{{reason_now}}`.

## Subject lines

Warm, picking-back-up, not a fresh pitch.

- circling back, properly this time
- {{company_name}} + us, take two

## Touches

Two touches.

- **Touch 1** - the reopen: the prior interaction and the reason-now. 2 variants.
- **Touch 2** - the follow-up, a fresh result or the update. 2 variants.

## Template

**Touch 1**

> Hey {{first_name}}, we spoke back {{last_touch}} about {{area}}.
>
> {one line of context}. Since then, {{reason_now}}.
>
> {a low-friction CTA}

**Touch 2** - the follow-up, body only, threads under Touch 1

> {a fresh result, or the improved offer} {a lighter re-ask}
