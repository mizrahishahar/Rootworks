Ouch — is it my voice, or just one of those rough days in moving?

(Opener2)

“Hey, I know this isn’t your dream call today — but if you give me 30 seconds, I’ll make sure it’s not your worst either.”

(Opener3)

“Hey, if I told you this call could make your moving company’s life easier — would that make it a little less cold?”

(Adjust tone depending on their reaction, then continue.)

If they say “No, go ahead”:

Perfect! Give me just 30 seconds to explain why I’m calling — and if it’s not something you’re even slightly interested in, I’ll disappear for the rest of the year. Deal?

The Story:

So here’s the deal — my boss, Sam, owns a moving company here in Canada. A few years ago, he hired a software team to build a custom CRM for his company.

After he started using it, his company grew from 1 location in Calgary with 2 trucks and a few movers to 5 major cities, 15 trucks, and over 70 employees — all within 4 years.

After seeing the results, he decided to make the CRM available to other moving companies — and that’s how I ended up on the phone with you today.

Sam is looking to meet with owners and managers of moving companies across Canada. Are you the owner or manager of this company?

If they say “NO”:

Got it. And what’s your position here, [Name]?

(After their answer, you can adapt depending on influence level — maybe ask for the best way to reach the decision-maker.)

⸻

If they say “YES”:

Perfect. Are you currently using any CRM system?

(Regardless of their answer):

Sam would love to meet with you — not just to show MovePlnr, but to talk business. He’s all about sharing ideas, connecting owners, and building partnerships in the industry.

Even if you never buy the CRM, you’ll gain insights and a potential business ally who’s facing the same challenges you are.

Which day would be best to book you for a quick video call — this week or next?

Totally fair — but may I ask, are you 100% satisfied with your business right now, with no room for improvement at all?

That’s amazing — seriously, I wish you continued success!
