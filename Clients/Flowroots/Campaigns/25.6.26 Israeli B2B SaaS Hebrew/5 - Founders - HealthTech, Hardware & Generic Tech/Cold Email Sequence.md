# Campaign 5: Founders x HealthTech, Hardware & Generic Tech

**Segment:** FR Vertical = Health & Life Sciences + Hardware & IoT + Generic B2B Tech | FR Persona = Founders / CEOs / Owners
**Deliverable wording:** "שיחות מכירה" (mixed segment: devices / hardware / pharma do not "demo").
**Structure:** Email 1 = core offer. Email 2 = follow-up angles (anti-retainer / too-good-to-be-true). Money results-based, not "only". Light CTAs, no call ask, no referral.

---
