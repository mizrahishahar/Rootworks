# Partnership

Not a sale, a referral channel. Reach adjacent, non-competing providers who already serve your ICP from another angle: a CPA to your bookkeeping, a designer to your dev. The ask is a relationship, and it compounds.

## The play

Peer to peer, never a pitch.

Open on the shared market from a different angle ("we serve the same {{ICP}} from a different angle"), as equals trading, not selling. The mutual fit in one line and the incentive, with rev-share lowering the friction to say yes. Keep it a quick chat, no deck. Runs evergreen: nurture the partners every 30 days and the deal flow compounds.

## Needs

- A list of adjacent, non-competing providers serving the same ICP, principals only.
- A decided incentive: two-way referrals, or a rev-share.
- Var: `{{adjacent_angle}}`.

## Subject lines

Peer-toned, partnership-framed.

- referral partnership, {{first_name}}?
- same {{ICP}}, different angle

## Touches

Two touches.

- **Touch 1** - the peer partnership ask. 2 variants.
- **Touch 2** - the follow-up, a partner result or the incentive again. 2 variants.

## Template

**Touch 1**

> Hey {{first_name}}, we serve the same {{ICP}} you do, just from the {{adjacent_angle}} side.
>
> Curious if there's room for a simple referral partnership, two-way or a rev-share, whichever's cleaner for you.
>
> Worth a quick chat?

**Touch 2** - the follow-up, body only, threads under Touch 1

> {a partner result, or the incentive again} {a lighter re-ask}

## Examples

>
> We serve the same market from a different angle and send a fair bit of business out.
>
> Curious if there's an opportunity to set up a simple referral partnership?
