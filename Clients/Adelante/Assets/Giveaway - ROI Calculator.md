## What it does

Four inputs, all of which a founder knows off the top of their head:

1. Monthly support tickets
2. % that are repetitive (WISMO, returns, order edits)
3. Average handle time per ticket (minutes)
4. Fully loaded cost per agent hour

Returns: repetitive tickets/month, agent hours consumed, and **annual cost**.

**Default scenario** - 2,000 tickets/month, 60% repetitive, 8 min handle time, $28/hour - lands on **$53,760/year** burned on repetitive support.
