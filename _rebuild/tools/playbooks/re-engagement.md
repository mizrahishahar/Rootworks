# Re-engagement

They already raised a hand once: a no-show, a closed-lost, a stalled deal. Not cold, and it must not read cold. The frame is picking back up, not meeting for the first time. Reworks pull 7-14% because the relationship already exists.

## The play

- **The frame** — prior interaction, then one line of context, then the offer, then a reason-now, then a low-friction CTA.
- **The reason-now is the engine** — something changed on our side that makes now different: "we just helped a similar {{niche}} book 40 in six weeks", or a new capability. Without it, it is just a nag.
- **The context must be true** — they remember the last conversation, so a wrong detail kills it.
- Runs evergreen, on a quarterly export. Validate emails first; people changed jobs since.
