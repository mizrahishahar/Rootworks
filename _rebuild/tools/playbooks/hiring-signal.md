# Hiring-signal

A job post is a company telling you where it hurts. Anchor to the exact role and the gap it leaves open right now. Reach the decision-maker who owns the outcome, never the recruiter filling the seat.

## The play

- **Name the role** — "saw {{company}} just opened a {{role}}." Specific, current, unmistakably not mass mail.
- **The ramp-gap pain** — "instead of waiting three months for a new hire to ramp, {{outcome}} could be handled this week." The offer is the faster path to what the hire is for.
- **CTA** — start now, before the seat is filled and the pain is masked.
- Aim by company size: a founder at a small shop, a Head or Director at mid, a VP at enterprise. The recruiter is a dead end.
