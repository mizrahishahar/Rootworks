## FAQ (answer in your own words, asset as backup)
- Is this a CRM? No. It is the operating system for the whole operation (quoting, dispatch, payroll, follow-up, analytics); the CRM is one slice.
- We already have software. Most who switch did too; it replaces the CRM plus WhatsApp, quoting docs, and the dispatch board a generic tool does not touch.
- Multiple locations / long distance? Yes, a confirmed strength; priced per location, single all-hubs dispatch board.
- Cost? Free up to 10 moves a month; Starter 100 (30); Growth 249 (100, most popular); Scale 500 (unlimited). Per location, no contract. Only when asked.
- Who is Sam? Founder and CEO, built MovePlnr running Cactus Moving; the demo is with the mover who built the tool.
