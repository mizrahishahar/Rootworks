# Question-led

One sharp question about their world, and the whole email turns on it. Use when the pain is nameable and you want the lowest-friction reply, where a one-word answer counts.

## The play

- **The question is the opener**, specific and about them: "still handling {{task}} in-house?" or "who owns {{outcome}} over there?" A real question a real person would ask, never a rhetorical or a "did you know".
- **Body** — one line of context, then the offer framed as the answer to the question. Short.
- **CTA** — soft: a yes or no, or the asset. The question already lowered the bar; do not raise it.
- **Follow-ups** — reframe the question a different way, route, value.
