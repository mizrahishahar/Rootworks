## The shape

| | |
|---|---|
| Who | Papaya - Israeli footwear. **60+ physical stores** plus ecommerce. |
| Volume | **1,000+ customer inquiries.** |
| Before | Manual, fragmented service across channels with a small team. Response lag at peak (evenings, weekends) - customers followed up within *minutes*. Hiring more agents was operationally impractical. |
| After | **200-400 of every 1,000 inquiries close with zero human involvement.** Team refocused on the inquiries that need judgment. |
