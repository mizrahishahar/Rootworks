# Company Overrides — Piper AI

The few things that bend how the inbox manager operates for Piper AI. Everything else it handles on its own from the skill, the Assets, and the onboarding form.

- Never write "AI tool" or "generic AI." Piper's whole positioning is to escape that bucket, and a Copilot/ChatGPT comparison is the objection that kills it. Lead the copy with the citation angle: every output cited back to the source page.
- Booking runs on HubSpot, which isn't connected here, so a booking can't be verified through any tool. Don't claim a call is locked until the client confirms the invite went out.
