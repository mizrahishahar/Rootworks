# Dave.io — LinkedIn Hiring Campaign

*Outreach sequence for review · 2026-07-22*

## The campaign

- **Audience:** infra decision-makers (Founder / CTO / VP Eng / Head of Infra) at US companies that just posted an infrastructure hire.
- **Signal:** we reach them off a live infra-hiring post — so every message is one-to-one, never mass.
- **Two versions (A/B):** same pain, two different openings. Version A goes straight at the cost; Version B gives an easy out first. We split the audience and let the replies tell us which wins.
- **Personalization:** `{{firstName}}` and `{{companyName}}` fill in per person.

## Version A — direct

**Connection request:** *(blank — no note)*

**Message 1 — on accept**
> Hey {{firstName}}, I saw you're hiring on the infra side. How much of your engineers' week is actually going to infra right now, instead of building the product?

**Message 2 — follow-up**
> The deploys, the pipeline, the random fires. I'm guessing all of it lands on the people you'd rather have building the product?

**Message 3 — close**
> If it's handled, ignore me {{firstName}}. If not, we help you scale without adding DevOps headcount, so your engineers stay on the product. Want me to map where it'd help most at {{companyName}}?
