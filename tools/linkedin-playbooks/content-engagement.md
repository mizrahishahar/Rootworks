# Content-engagement

They are already deep in the topic; you meet them there. An evergreen play on people who like or comment on a set of creators whose audience is your ICP. The topic is the hook. The creator never is, since naming who you scraped is creepy and burns the play.

## The play

Hook the topic, not the person.

Hook the topic: "noticed you've been deep in {{topic}} lately" lands; "saw you commented on {{creator}}'s post" does not. Assume the soft intent and give the out: "are you looking at {{outcome}} this quarter, or just following the space." Stay light, one idea, low pressure, because a hard pitch on a soft signal reads as a stretch.

## Needs

- A chosen set of creators whose engaged audience is the ICP.
- A weekly scrape of engagers, deduped by profile, DNC applied.
- Vars: `{{topic}}`, `{{outcome}}`.

## Touches

Connection-gated.

- **Connection request** - blank.
- **Message 1** - the topic, noticed, and the soft question. 2 variants.
- **Message 2** - a light nudge or a piece of value. 2 variants.

## Template

**Connection request** - blank

**Message 1** (on accept)

> Hey {{first_name}}, noticed you've been deep in {{topic}} lately.
>
> Curious if you're looking at {{outcome}} this quarter, or just following the space.
>
> {a soft, low-pressure question}

**Message 2** (follow-up)

> {a light piece of value tied to the topic, then the out}

## Examples

> Hey {{first_name}}, noticed you've been engaging with a lot of {{topic}} content lately.
>
> Curious if you're exploring ways to {{outcome}} this quarter, or just keeping tabs on the space?
