# Content-engagement

They are already deep in the topic; you meet them there. An evergreen play on people who like or comment on a set of creators whose audience is your ICP. The topic is the hook. The creator never is, since naming who you scraped is creepy and burns the play.

## The play

- **Hook the topic, not the person** — "noticed you've been deep in {{topic}} lately" lands; "saw you commented on {{creator}}'s post" does not. Reference the theme they keep engaging with, as if you simply noticed the pattern.
- **Assume the intent, give the out** — engagement is soft intent, so frame the offer as the thing they are already circling: "curious if you're looking at {{outcome}} this quarter, or just following the space." The out lowers the guard.
- **Stay light** — these did not fill a form. One idea, low pressure, a soft question CTA. A hard pitch on a soft signal reads as a stretch.
- **Evergreen cadence** — the list refills weekly, so the copy is a standing template. Rotate {{topic}} and {{outcome}} to the week's creator set; keep the frame.
