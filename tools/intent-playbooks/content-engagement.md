# Content-engagement

They are already deep in the topic; you meet them there. An evergreen play on people who like or comment on a set of creators whose audience is your ICP. The topic is the hook. The creator never is, since naming who you scraped is creepy and burns the play.

## Needs

- A chosen set of creators whose engaged audience is the ICP.
- A weekly scrape of engagers, deduped by profile, DNC applied.
- Vars: `{{topic}}`, `{{outcome}}`.

## The play

Hook the topic, not the person: "noticed you've been deep in {{topic}} lately" lands; "saw you commented on {{creator}}'s post" does not. Assume the soft intent and give the out: "curious if you're looking at {{outcome}} this quarter, or just following the space." Stay light, one idea, low pressure, a soft question CTA, because a hard pitch on a soft signal reads as a stretch. The list refills weekly, so the copy is a standing template: rotate `{{topic}}` and `{{outcome}}` to the week's creator set, keep the frame.

## Subject lines

The subjects below are examples; write fresh to the play.

Topic-led, curious, never naming the creator.
- "{{topic}} question"
- "you + {{topic}}"

## The plan

| Variant | Opener | Offer | Proof / risk reversal | CTA |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1A | the topic, noticed | the outcome they're circling | light | are you exploring it |
| 1B | a topic question | the offer as the answer | light | just following, or acting |
