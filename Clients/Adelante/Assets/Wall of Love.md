## Why this carries more than a testimonial

A testimonial is a client saying nice things because you asked. This is **a stranger, mid-problem, unprompted, breaking off to check.** Nobody asks a bot if it is a bot. They ask because the answer surprised them.

It is also the answer to the deepest objection in this market - *"my customers will know, and they'll hate it"* - and it answers it without an argument. Papaya says the same thing from the other side: *"Even though it says it's a bot, people think they're talking to a real person."*
