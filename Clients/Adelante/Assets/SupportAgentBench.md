# SupportAgentBench - the public benchmark

**Use: SEND.** Authority, not proof. Know the difference before you send it.

**Send:** `https://www.getadelante.com/eval` · methodology: `/eval/methodology`

---

## 🔴 Read this first

**The benchmark does not include Adelante.** It scores **26 LLMs** - GPT-5.x, Claude/Sonnet, Gemini, Grok, DeepSeek, Kimi, Qwen, GLM - as autonomous support agents. Adelante is not on the leaderboard. It is not a Adelante score.

**Never send it as "our benchmark result".** That invites the one question we cannot answer: *"so where are you on it?"*

**Send it as: we publish the benchmark this whole category gets measured by.** That claim is true, and it is worth more.

## What it actually is

- **162 grounded support conversations**: 120 routine, 24 must-escalate, 18 adversarial traps built to catch unsafe behaviour
- Multi-turn, up to 8 exchanges · English · **median of 3 runs** · last updated **July 2026**
- Measures: **human handoff %** (over-escalation), **forbidden actions** triggered (0-2 safe, 3-5 mixed, 6+ reckless), **escalation accuracy %**, **resolution %**, **policy adherence**, **cost tier** per 1,000 conversations
- Per-model pages under `/eval/models/*`

**Findings worth quoting:** best autonomy Grok-4.3 (2.5% handoff) · best quality GPT-5.5 (100% escalation accuracy, top cost tier) · best value Gemma-4-31b (10% handoff, only 2 forbidden actions, cheapest tier).

## Who to send it to

The **skeptic** and the **technical evaluator** - the prospect who says "everyone claims AI support works" or who has been burned by a bot before. This is the asset that says: *we are the people who measured it, rigorously enough to publish the numbers, including the ones that make our own category look bad.*

Wrong for a founder who just wants their queue handled. Right for the person blocking the deal.

## The angle it unlocks

*"We ran 26 models through 162 support conversations - 18 of them adversarial traps. Here is what we found."*

That is a genuinely useful, non-salesy thing to say to a stranger, and almost nobody in this market can say it. It earns a reply on its own merit rather than asking for one.
