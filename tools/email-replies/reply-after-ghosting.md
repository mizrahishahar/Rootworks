# Reply after ghosting

The prospect replies to one of the follow-ups. Back to the machine - the personalised outcome paragraph and the natural next step, now grounded in the fuller thread.

## When
A lead we had followed up (and who had gone quiet) answers again. Treat the reply as a fresh First reply, richer for everything the thread now holds.

## The play
1. **Answer what they said** with value.
2. **Outcome, personalised** - tie to the angle they replied to, add something the thread has not spent.
3. **Natural reason the call is the next step**, then two windows in their timezone, and invite their own time (same mechanic as First reply).

## Never
Treat the return as a reset that forgets the thread; re-use a hook already spent; act surprised or grateful that they came back.

## Example
After two quiet follow-ups, an owner replied "sorry, been buried - what's the gist?":

> All good - the gist is speed. Every lead off your site gets a quote back in seconds instead of hours, so none slip while a crew is mid-move, and that alone moved Sam's own shop past $7M.
>
> Fastest way to see it on your setup is a walkthrough with Sam. Tuesday 1pm or Wednesday 10am CT - which works? Or name a time and I'll set it up.

Answers the actual sentence, lands a fresh outcome number, then the natural next step with two real times and the own-time invite.
