# No-show

The call time passed and they did not show. Rebook, more assertive. Make it understood they no-showed and that our time deserves respect, without a guilt trip.

## When
A booked call the prospect missed. Move within the day while the intent is still warm.

## The play
1. **Name that we missed each other** - plainly, no drama.
2. **One reason it is still worth it** - the outcome, fresh.
3. **Two specific times, no invite-your-own-time.** Non-needy is the whole message.

## Never
Guilt trips; "you missed"; asking what happened; apologising for them; inviting their own time this round.

## Example
An owner no-showed the 1pm:

> Looks like we missed each other at 1 - happens. The reason it's worth grabbing again: Sam maps the quote-speed piece straight onto your crews, and that's where the money he found in his own shop lives. He's open tomorrow 11am or Thursday 2pm PT - which works?

Names the miss without blame, gives one fresh reason, two specific times, no own-time invite. Non-needy throughout.
