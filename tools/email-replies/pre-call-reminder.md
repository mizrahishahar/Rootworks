# Pre-call reminder

The timing touch that keeps the call top of mind. A reminder, never bare.

## When
Close to the call: a day-before touch on a longer gap, and always a morning-of touch.

## The play
A short, human line that confirms the time in their timezone and carries one sentence of anticipation or a single thing to bring, so it reads as a person looking forward to it, not a system firing a reminder.

## Never
"Reminder: call at 2"; a guilt nudge; re-asking the time.

## Example
Morning of a 2pm call:

> Morning John - still set for 2pm PT with Sam. He'll have your quote-speed numbers pulled up, so come with the one bottleneck you'd most want gone.

Confirms the time, gives him a reason to be ready, no bare reminder.
