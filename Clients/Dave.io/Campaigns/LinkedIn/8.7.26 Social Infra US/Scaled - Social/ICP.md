# Scaled - Social - ICP

**Segment:** scaled but bottlenecked. VP Engineering / Head of Infrastructure / Director of DevOps at larger, growth-stage US companies with a stretched infra team, sourced from engaging with infra content on LinkedIn.

## Social discovery - Search Posts

Time range: Last Month (refresh weekly)
Keywords (max 6): platform engineering, developer productivity, cloud cost, scaling infrastructure, DevOps as a service, SRE
Engagement types: Comments, Reactions, Shares
Add later, sharper than keywords: Specific Creators + Specific Companies (competitor pages), names TBD

## Audience - the prompt to find the right people

From the people who engaged with those posts, keep only the senior engineering leader at scaled, cloud-based US software companies: VP Engineering, Head of Infrastructure, Head of Platform Engineering, Director of DevOps, or Director of SRE, at VP/Director/Head seniority in engineering, at 200 to 2000 employee companies (Series B and beyond), running on AWS, GCP, or Azure, headquartered in the United States. Do not keep individual engineers, DevOps hires, or founders of tiny startups. One person per company.

## Structured backup (filters)


Company:
  headcount:    201-2000
  funding:      Series B, Series C, Series D+
  technologies: AWS OR GCP OR Azure
  hq_location:  United States
Contact:
  seniority:      VP, Director, Head
  department:     Engineering (leadership only, no Engineer/Developer/SRE/DevOps sub-departments)
  exclude titles: Engineer, Developer only  (NOT SRE or DevOps - those remove Director of DevOps/SRE)
Strictness:       strict on persona + location, flexible on keywords + engagement

(No industry filter. No hiring gate, the topic engagement is the signal. No strict job-title-contains, it over-narrows a social audience.)
