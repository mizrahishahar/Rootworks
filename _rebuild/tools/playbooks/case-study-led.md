# Case-study-led

Proof carries the pitch. Lead with a matched result instead of a claim. Use in low-trust niches, or when the outcome reads too good and you hold a close case study. Roofer to roofer, SaaS to SaaS: the tighter the match, the harder it lands.

## The play

- **Open on the match** — "worked with a {{niche}} about your size", so the number that follows is believable before they reach it.
- **The result in plain numbers** — "took them from $25k to $250k in 90 days." One result, concrete, closest to their world. Never invent or inflate it; if the case is not in the brief, this playbook does not apply.
- **The offer follows the proof**, never before it: the proof earns the right to pitch.
- **CTA** — offer the breakdown of how, or the same result built for them.
- **Follow-ups** — a second proof from a different angle, then route, then value.
