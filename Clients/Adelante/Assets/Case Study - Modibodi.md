## The shape

| | |
|---|---|
| Who | Modibodi - period underwear. Global brand, this deployment is **Israel**. |
| Before | Every solution they tried felt robotic. Rigid **"button bot"** experiences that created friction instead of helping people through a complex, personal buying decision. Needed Shopify + inventory + marketing integrations while holding a sensitive tone - **especially in Hebrew**. Nothing on the market met the bar. |
| What we did | Natural-conversation agent, integrated with Shopify, order data, inventory, customer history. Trained on the brand's precise, sensitive tone. |
| After | **50% of inquiries resolved with no human representative.** Instant answers outside working hours. |
