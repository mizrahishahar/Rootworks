# Local

For local-service businesses, proximity is the easiest opener there is. You are a neighbor who noticed them, not a vendor blasting a list. Plain and human beats slick every time here.

## The play

- **Open on the locality** — "saw your {{business}} over in {{city}}, looks like you're doing solid work." Conversational, a little warm.
- **Body** — a nearby proof in their own terms ("helped another shop in {{city}} book 15 jobs in month one"), then the plain offer. No overclaiming, no corporate polish; trades smell it instantly.
- **CTA** — casual, the tone of a referral between locals.
- Weighted to a cold call with the same locality hook; the short email backs it up.
