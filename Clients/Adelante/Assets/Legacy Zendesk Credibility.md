## 🔴 The fence

Adelante was a **Zendesk implementation consultancy before it was an AI agent company.** These case studies are from that era - 2020, Zendesk migrations and automation builds. They are **not** the managed AI support agent the client is selling today.

**Never cite Terminal X or HTzone as proof that the AI agent works.** It is a different product, from six years ago. The AI-agent proof is Outlier Group, Fold, Modibodi, Papaya - and only those.

**What these are good for:** the credibility line. *"Adelante has been building support operations for Israeli ecommerce since 2020 - Terminal X, HTzone."* That is true, and for an IL prospect those are names they know.
