# Tye Frankel

A relationship-first LinkedIn DM framework. Not a pitch sequence, a conversation: you throw rope, build trust, and let an invited opening carry you to a call. Manual, 5-15 messages, 4-6/10 enthusiasm throughout. Use for hand-picked, high-value ICP where a booked call is worth an hour of real conversation.

## Needs

- An optimized profile (the landing page that pre-sells) and a light content presence.
- 2-3 proprietary phrases, each tied to a specific ICP pain, that make a prospect ask "what do you mean?"
- 1-2 sales assets to drop contextually (a case study, a short video, a doc).

## The play

- **Pre-DM touchpoints** on every accepted connection: endorse two skills, like a post, comment tagging their name, follow the company page. Signals human, not bot.
- **Multi-thread opening**, sent as separate short messages, no periods (casual, non-needy). Mix personal and business: "Hey {{first_name}}" / a specific personal note / a specific business note / a soft pain-point seed ("is {{company}} just you or do you have a team?").
- **Restrained compliments, 4-6/10** - "solid case study, 3.2X ROI is good for e-com FB ads," never "I love your work!" Specificity proves research; restraint signals you have seen better.
- **Mirror, resonate, reflect, then ask** - acknowledge the reply, relate naturally, then the next question. Never interrogate.
- **Authority observations, not pitches** - drop credibility in their context: "most agencies here have margin problems, you need 60-80% to scale."
- **Pry pain points** gently throughout; a small signal is enough to pry wide open.
- **The "what do you mean?" cheat code** - drop a proprietary phrase tied to their pain ("any backend upside on your offer?"). They ask what it is. Define it, link it to a common problem, drop proof, soft-pitch the call: "would be down to run you through it sometime." Never "let me know if you want to book!"

## Subject lines

None; LinkedIn DMs have no subject.

## The plan

| Touch | Move | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| pre-DM | endorse, like, comment, follow | signal human |
| opener | multi-thread: personal + business + pain seed | earn a reply |
| build | restrained compliment, mirror, authority observation | build trust |
| pry | pain-point questions, woven in | find the wedge |
| pitch | the proprietary-phrase opening, then a soft call ask | the invited pitch |

## Templates

**Multi-thread opener** (separate messages)
> Hey {{first_name}}
> {a specific personal note}
> {a specific business note}
> {{company}} just you, or do you have a team?

**The invited pitch**
> Most {{ICP}} have {{pain}}, which makes it hard to {{goal}}. Big reason I {{proof}}. Would be down to run you through it sometime.

## Examples

From the framework:
- Opener: "Hey [Name]" / "Crazy video jumping out of the plane, you're brave." / "I like the concept behind [Company], solid." / "Is [Company] just you or do you have a team?"
- Pitch: "Most agency owners have low margins, so it's hard to scale. You need 60-80% to hire A players. Big reason I scaled both my agencies past 100K a month. Would be down to run you through it sometime."
