# Partnership

Not a sale, a referral channel. You reach adjacent, non-competing providers who already serve your ICP from another angle: a CPA to your bookkeeping, a designer to your dev. The ask is a relationship, and it compounds.

## The play

- **Peer to peer, never a pitch** — "we serve the same {{ICP}} from a different angle. Worth setting up a simple referral partnership?" You are equals trading, not selling.
- **Body** — the mutual fit in one line, and the incentive: two-way referrals, or a rev-share (rev-share lowers their friction to say yes).
- **CTA** — a quick chat about referrals, no deck.
- Runs evergreen: nurture the partners every 30 days and the deal flow compounds.
