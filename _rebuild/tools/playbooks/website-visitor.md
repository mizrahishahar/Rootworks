# Website-visitor

Someone from their company looked at our site. High intent, but noisy: identity resolution guesses, so an unfiltered callout to a non-buyer is the embarrassing false positive. Filter to ICP first, always.

## The play

- **Light and low-pressure** — "saw someone from {{company}} checking us out, are you looking into {{problem}} right now or was it just research?" Give the out; it disarms the creep factor.
- **Split-test transparent vs silent** — "saw you visited" against simply opening on {{problem}} with no mention of the visit. Silent often wins.
- **Body** — the offer, no pressure.
- Runs evergreen, auto-enrolling ICP-matched visitors.
