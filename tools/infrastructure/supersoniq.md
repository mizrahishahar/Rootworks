# Supersoniq

Contact database, strong where buyers are on LinkedIn: corporate, GTM, funded, mid-market and up. Caps on owner-operated, local, and trades. Also the terminus for a company-discovery pull, through the domain bridge. No software or SaaS label exists in its filters; the niche is isolated after a pull from the industry tags.

## Actions (MCP)
- Free: `get_key_info` (balance), `count_contacts` (an estimate; a round number, especially 1000, means larger), `get_filter_values`, `match_companies`, `search_contacts` in preview mode.
- Pull: `search_contacts` full - 1 credit with the email, 0.5 naked; pull with the email when these people will be emailed.
- Bridge: `enrich_companies` - decision-makers from domains, ~200 domains per call.

## Spend gate
Ceiling = domains x contacts per company, at 1 credit per delivered contact. Size with `count_contacts`, read the balance with `get_key_info`, state the ceiling before any pull.
