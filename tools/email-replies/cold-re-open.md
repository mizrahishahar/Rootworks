# Cold re-open

A lead quiet for weeks, beyond the follow-up rhythm. Restart the human thread - a simple nudge or a genuine question about their world. Never repeat the call ask.

## When
An interested lead that aged out of the active follow-up cadence (weeks quiet). The re-engagement move, not another follow-up.

## The play
A simple, human re-open: a genuine question about their world, or a light nudge tied to something real that changed. The goal is a reply and a live thread again, nothing more. Once they answer, it becomes Reply after ghosting.

## Never
Repeat the call ask; a slot menu; neediness; a recap of the old pitch; "just circling back."

## Example
A mover quiet for three weeks after early interest:

> Hey {first_name} - season's probably peaking for you. Curious, did the quote-turnaround thing ever get sorted on your end, or still going out by hand?

A real question about their world, no ask, no pitch - just enough to restart the conversation.
